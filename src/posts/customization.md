#  Personalize Your Deck 

Ready to make your Steam Deck truly yours? Let's talk about **Decky Loader**. 

## What is Decky Loader? 
It's an amazing, community-made tool called **[Decky Loader](https://decky.xyz/)** that lets you add *plugins* to your SteamOS Game Mode. Think of it like putting stickers on your laptop, but these stickers actually do things.

### How to Install (Simple Version)
1. Switch to **Desktop Mode**.
2. Go to the [Decky Loader](https://decky.xyz) website.
3. Download and run the installer.
4. Set your 'admin password' (if you haven't yet).
5. Restart your Deck!

## Our Favorite Plugins
Once you've installed Decky, a new 'Plug' icon will appear in your **... Menu**. Click it, and you can browse for:
- **CSS Loader**: The absolute best. It lets you change colors, fonts, and even the shape of the Steam buttons.
- **[SteamGridDB](https://www.steamgriddb.com/)**: Lets you swap the artwork of your games. Found a game that has a boring grey cover? Replace it with a cool animated one!
- **Bluetooth**: Makes connecting your AirPods or Xbox controllers way faster.
- **HLTB (How Long To Beat)**: Puts a timer on your game page, showing you how long it takes to finish the game.

## Making it Look Modern
Check out these 'themes' in CSS Loader:
- **Clean Game View**: Strips away the clutter and shows more of your game library.
- **Round Corners**: Makes the UI look more modern and sleek, similar to iOS or Windows 11.
- **Classic Blue**: Gives you those 'old-school' Steam vibes if you're a fan of the 2010s look.
