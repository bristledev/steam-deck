#  Beyond Steam (The Whole World on Your Deck) 

The Steam Deck is a PC. That means you can play games from **Epic Games Store, GOG, Ubisoft**, and even **EA**. 

## The Heroic Games Launcher 
Instead of fighting with these other stores' official apps (which often struggle on Linux), the community built the **[Heroic Games Launcher](https://heroicgameslauncher.com/)**. It’s the easiest way to manage your **[Epic Games](https://www.epicgames.com/)** and **[GOG](https://www.gog.com/)** libraries on the Steam Deck!

1. Go to **Desktop Mode**.
2. Open **Discover**.
3. Search 'Heroic' and click **Install**.
4. Log in!

Once it's installed, you can download all your GOG and Epic games. After downloading, you can even add them to your Steam library so you don't even have to leave **Game Mode** to play them!

## Emulation: The EmuDeck Magic 
If you want to play games from your childhood (Nintendo, PlayStation, etc.), **[EmuDeck](https://www.emudeck.com/)** is exactly what you need. 

1. Head to [EmuDeck.com](https://emudeck.com).
2. Download the installer.
3. Run it and choose 'Easy Mode'.
4. It'll automatically set up all the complicated stuff for you.

*Disclaimer*: Emulation is legal, but downloading games you don't own (ROMs) is not. Make sure you use your own legally-owned backups!

## Non-Steam Games (The 'Secret' Menu)
You can even add regular programs to Steam.
1. In Steam (Desktop Mode), click **Games** -> **Add a Non-Steam Game...**
2. Find the app you want (like Discord or Firefox).
3. Now it shows up in your library like any other game!

That’s it! You’ve gone from a SteamOS ‘Noob’ to someone who knows more than 90% of most people who own a Deck. But the journey doesn't end here!
